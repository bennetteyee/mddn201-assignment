Seeing Networks in Wellington

Examples of networks I saw whilst walking around Wellington.

Manhole Covers

Manholes are spotted throughout Wellington, they are used for various things. They are used to carry out inspection, cleaning and removing obstructions in the sewer line.

Electrical Boxes

Electrical boxes connects businesses and people to power. Through the need of electricity in our daily lives we are connected.

Surveillance

CCTV cameras are in place in public places for crime prevention. The objective of installing the cameras is to reduce the incidence of crime and disorder, so members of the community feel safe.
